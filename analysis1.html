Bruce Highway Crash Heatmap

The most dangerous road in Australia.


I was interested in crash data in Queensland as I was curious which road was the most dangerous. I was unable to find government resources to suit, therefore i made my own using government provided datasets.

First, I took the dataset to R for cleaning. During cleaning I removed property damage as the government discontinued tracking it throughout the dataset and found the most dangerous road in Queensland by:

Once the most dangerous road was found, I rebuilt the dataset with only the values from the Bruce highway and moved to Tableau.

Once in Tableau, I wanted to create something anyone could use. This is why i chose buttons with dropdown to handle filtering; reducing clutter. Colours blue and orange are also chosen to prevent colourblindness from inhibiting the view.
